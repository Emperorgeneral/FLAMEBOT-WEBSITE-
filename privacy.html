Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-22

This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use FlameBot.

Back to homepage

Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service and support your account.

  • Account identifiers: FlameBot ID, license key/status, and related service metadata.
  • Telegram-related identifiers: Telegram user ID and verification artifacts needed to link your account (we do not request your Telegram password).
  • Operational telemetry: timestamps of registration/verification steps, server request metadata, and limited error logs.
  • Trading automation state: EA settings you choose to sync (e.g., lot mode/symbol lists) and trade-state summaries used to operate the automation.

Note: the FlameBot desktop app stores some session/config data locally on your device (for example in your user profile folder). That local data is controlled by your OS account.

Operator and Contact

FlameBot is operated by FLAME CORE TECHNOLOGIES LTD. For privacy questions or requests, contact us via the Contact page.

How We Use Data

  • Provide and authenticate access to FlameBot features.
  • Sync settings between the desktop app and the server service.
  • Help troubleshoot support requests and improve reliability.
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.

How Data Is Stored

  • Server data is stored in managed infrastructure (typically a database) with access controls.
  • We do not intentionally store plaintext Telegram passwords.
  • Payment details (like full card numbers) are handled by payment processors; we do not store full payment card data on our servers.

We retain account and operational data only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You can request data access or deletion via the contact page.

Cookies

This website is a static site and does not require cookies for basic browsing.

  • We do not use analytics cookies on this website at this time.
  • If third-party widgets or analytics are enabled in the future, they may set cookies and this policy will be updated.

Account Data Protection

  • We use access controls and secret management to protect server credentials.
  • We aim to minimize sensitive data and store only what is needed to operate the service.
  • You are responsible for protecting access to your computer, MetaTrader terminal, and broker account.

Your Choices

  • You can request support or data questions via the contact page.
  • Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data.
