About FlameBot

FlameBot is built to make Telegram-to-MetaTrader automation reliable, auditable, and easy to operate — without hand-copying trades.

Back to homepage

Purpose

We build tooling that turns human-readable trading signals (typically posted in Telegram groups/channels) into structured, repeatable automation workflows that can be executed and controlled from MetaTrader.

Mission

Deliver a professional-grade desktop companion that helps traders deploy automation with clear configuration, safe defaults, and transparent system behavior.

Vision

A simple automation stack for retail trading: consistent signal ingestion, consistent execution rules, and consistent operational visibility — regardless of broker, account type, or market session.

  • Operational focus: reduce errors from manual execution.
  • Technical focus: predictable automation, not black-box promises.
  • Security focus: protect account identifiers and minimize stored secrets.
